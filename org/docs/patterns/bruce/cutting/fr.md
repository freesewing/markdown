Bruce consist of a back, and two sides, insets and fronts.

 - **Main fabric**
   - Couper **1 dos** au pli
   - Cut **2 fronts** on the fold
   - Cut **2 insets**
   - Cut **2 sides**

<Warning>

###### Caveats

- **back**: Extra hem allowance at the leg
- **front**: Cut this **twice**

</Warning>
